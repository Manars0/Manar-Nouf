Saudi Architecture

An integrated urban concept that aims to introduce authentic and diverse architecture in various regions of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, and to enhance it through design controls and guidelines based on achieving a balance between authenticity and modernity, so that the architectural and urban design process is directed towards contemporary solutions that reflect the aesthetic and heritage values of the Kingdom, thus contributing to the development of a .modern urban environment rooted in the local character

Royal Highness Prince Mohammed bin Salman "Saudi architecture represents a unique blend of ancient heritage and contemporary design, contributing to the attractiveness of cities, enhancing tourism and supporting sectors related to hospitality and construction"
